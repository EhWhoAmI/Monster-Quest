Monster Quest

A short introduction of the game

Welcome to Monster Quest! It is a place, where peaceful people live. The environment is beautiful, and there are no wars (yet). But, there was a curse by an evil wizard who wanted to take over the world. He conquered lots of places, but one day, he lost control of his army! So, from then since, the monster terrorised the innocent people of the land.

Gameplay

You take the form of a young fighter who is willing to fight in the village of NEWBIES TOWN. It is up to you, to help save the world!

Classes

There are 4 classes to choose from to fight with. They all have different types of gameplay.
